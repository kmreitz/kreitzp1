Kale and Mint Salad with Peanut Vinaigrette

I have been obsessed with this salad for weeks now! I didn't think I was a big kale fan but this salad really turned me into one.
I use peanut butter in the vinaigrette instead of cashew butter and I love it even more. The best part is, I usually will double the recipe and then I have a bunch of dressing for salads all week!

What's in the Peanut Vinaigrette?
- Fresh garlic and ginger
- Sesame oil
- Creamy peanut butter
- Rice vinegar
- Lime (or lemon) juice
- Chili oil
- Coconut Aminos
- Salt and pepper
